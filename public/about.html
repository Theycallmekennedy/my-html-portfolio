About Me

Kennedy Ezuruike Nnadozie is a Lagos-based creative whose work sits at the intersection of music production, social media strategy, and entrepreneurship. He moves comfortably between structure and spontaneity, which is why stepping into front-end development and digital marketing feels like a natural extension of everything he’s been building. He approaches projects as systems, stories, and sound — all working together.

He’s the mind behind Kendy Records and One or Two Enterprises, where he handles everything from production to creative direction. Whether he’s mixing vocals, planning a marketing rollout, or shaping the digital presence of restaurants and fashion brands, Kennedy brings the same mix of clarity, intention, and instinct. People trust him because he doesn’t just complete tasks — he understands the culture behind them.

At the core, Kennedy is driven by growth. He’s always learning, always pushing into new disciplines, and always looking for ways to elevate both his work and the people around him. Music, tech, storytelling — they all meet in his hands. That blend is what gives his craft its edge.
